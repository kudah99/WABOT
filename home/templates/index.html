Why choose Whatsapp

Internet usage statistics are reported by the Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (Potraz). We discovered from those statistics that WhatsApp accounted for over half of all internet usage in the nation. In extreme circumstances, some compare WhatsApp with the internet. They only know how to use the instant messaging software to satisfy their requirements for news, entertainment, and socializing by purchasing WhatsApp bundles. Some people are aware that the internet extends beyond WhatsApp, but they don't really need anything else. WhatsApp is without a doubt the best platform for connecting with Zimbabwe's millions of people.

What's this about?

Zimbabwe is set to hold elections in the near future, and it is important that citizens are informed about the electoral process. However, accessing information regarding polling stations, participating candidates, and voter registration can be challenging for many people. This is particularly true for those who speak local native languages or have limited internet access. To tackle this problem, we propose the development of a chatbot that will provide users with easy access to relevant information.
